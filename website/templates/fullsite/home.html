Booking Events

Racing Stadium

Immerse yourself in the excitement of horse and greyhound racing at our specialized racing stadium. Designed exclusively for these thrilling events, the stadium offers comfortable seating, clear track views, and a vibrant atmosphere for fans of all ages.

Basketball Courts

Our premium basketball courts are built for both casual play and professional games. Featuring high-quality flooring, adjustable hoops, and ample spectator seating, they provide an ideal setting for players and fans to enjoy the sport.

Football Pitch

Step onto a world-class football pitch designed to FIFA standards. Whether for practice sessions or competitive matches, the lush, well-maintained grass and excellent lighting make it a pitch to remember.

Concert Arena

The concert arena offers a vibrant space for live performances, with cutting-edge acoustics, dynamic lighting systems, and seating for thousands. Guests can also enjoy drinks and meals at our on-site bar and restaurant, adding to the ultimate entertainment experience.
